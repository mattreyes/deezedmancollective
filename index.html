About Us

The Deezed Man Collective (DMC) is a music collective from the suburban outskirts of Toronto, Canada. Influenced by an eclectic and groovy fusion of pop, R&B, blues, and jazz, their fresh sound can get any crowd to jam along.

Led by Anthony Nufrio (guitar/songwriting) and Matthew Reyes (drums/production), the DMC regularly records, produces, and performs in the Greater Toronto Area.

The DMC’s recent releases, Almost Alone, and Something Sweet are available on all major streaming platforms! Stay tuned on their latest adventures on Instagram @deezedmancollective and Facebook!


Before they were deezed...

In 2005, when childhood friends Anthony Nufrio and Matthew Reyes picked up their first instruments in grade school music class. Learning to cover their favourite songs and soundtracks, they developed an amazing chemistry playing together.

University had put them on separate paths for years, until in 2019 when they reunited to make music again as the Deezed Man Collective. Since then, they have been honing their musicianship and songwriting, growing to include Ramy Zhang on vocals, John Salazar on keys, and Ryan McCaul on bass.

Today, the DMC collaborates and produces with various artists from all over, including DeveyDGAF, VIBEBYMELO, Mila D, Ray Hickey Jr., kundimusic, and more!
